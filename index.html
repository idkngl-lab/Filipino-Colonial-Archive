Colonial Philippines

Exploring the struggles, resistance, and resilience of Filipinos during the Spanish and American colonial periods.

Explore History Visual Arts

Historical Struggles

For centuries, Filipinos lived under colonial rule. Heavy taxation, forced labor, social inequality, and restrictions on freedom shaped daily life. Despite these hardships, Filipinos continued to fight for independence and preserve their cultural identity.

Spanish Rule

More than 300 years of Spanish colonization influenced religion, education, and government while imposing hardships on many Filipinos.

Resistance

National heroes and revolutionary groups challenged colonial authority and inspired the movement for freedom.

American Period

The American era introduced new systems of education and governance while debates over independence continued.
